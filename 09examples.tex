We use the following notation for groups:

\begin{itemize}
\item $D_n$ - dihedral group of order $n$,
\item $S_n$ - symmetric group of degree $n$,
\item $C_n$ - cyclic group of order $n$,
\item $G x H$ - direct product of groups $G$ and $H$
\end{itemize}
